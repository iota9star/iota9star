## 👨‍💻 About Me

Hi! 👋 I'm **iota9star** — a full-stack developer who stubbornly refuses to choose between **speed at the metal** and **delight at the pixels**. 🧠⚡

By day, I architect resilient, high-throughput services in **Rust** and **Java**; by night, I ship polished **Flutter** apps and Dart packages into the open-source wild. To me, a great product is one story told end-to-end: a tight API contract, sub-millisecond latency, buttery animations, and developer experience that makes the next dev smile. 😊

- 🔭 **Now:** building fast, observable backends with Rust (Axum) and resilient microservices with Java/Spring.
- 🌱 **Exploring:** the bleeding edges of **AI agents**, **Web3 primitives**, and dev tooling that quietly removes friction.
- 🍡 **Off the clock:** deep in **anime seasons** 🎌 — a passion that sparked several of my most-loved projects!
- ⚡ **Brag-worthy:** a flagship repo sailing past **1.3k ⭐**, with ~1.7k stars earned across the board.
- ✉️ **Open inbox:** always happy to talk systems, Flutter, or wild side-project ideas — say hi anytime!
