## 👨‍💻 About Me

Hey! 👋 I'm **iota9star** — a builder who refuses to pick a lane between **raw performance at the metal** and **delight at the pixels**. 🧠⚡

I think of a product as one continuous story: a tidy API contract, sub-millisecond latency, animations that feel alive, and developer ergonomics that make the next person smile. 😊 By day I design resilient, high-throughput backends in **Rust** and **Java**; after hours I ship polished **Flutter** apps and Dart packages into the open-source wild.

- 🔭 **Right now:** crafting fast, observable services with Rust (Axum) and fault-tolerant microservices with Java/Spring. ⚙️
- 🌱 **Digging into:** the frontier of **AI agents**, **Web3 primitives**, and dev tooling that quietly removes friction. 🤖
- 🍡 **Off duty:** lost in **anime seasons** 🎌 — a love that quietly spawned several of my most-starred projects!
- ⚡ **Mild brag:** a flagship repo past **1.3k ⭐**, with roughly **1.7k stars** earned across everything. 🌟
- ✉️ **Inbox open:** always up for a chat about systems, Flutter, or a wild side project — say hi! 💬
